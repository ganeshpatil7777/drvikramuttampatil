About

Top Gastroenterologist, hepatologist and interventional endoscopist

Dr. Vikram Uttam Patil is a top Gastroenterologist, hepatologist and interventional endoscopist practicing in Pune, Maharashtra. He has done more than 3000 endoscopy which includes- Gastroscopy Colonoscopy, Enteroscopy, ERCP, EUS, Manometry and interventional therapeutics procedures. He is currently working at Viswaraj Multispecialty Hospital, Loni Kalbhor, Pune. He has published 10+ articles in various national and international journals.
