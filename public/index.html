About

Best Gastroenterologist, hepatologist and interventional endoscopist in Hadapsar, Pune.

Dr. Vikram Uttam Patil is a top Gastroenterologist, hepatologist and interventional endoscopist practicing in Hadapsar, Pune, Maharashtra. He has done more than 4000 endoscopy which includes- Gastroscopy, Colonoscopy, Enteroscopy, ERCP, EUS, Manometry and interventional therapeutics procedures. He is currently working at Vishwaraj Multispecialty Hospital, Loni Kalbhor, Pune. He has published 12+ articles in various national and international journals.
